Origins

Born from late-night sketches and found textures, the label blends folk horror motifs with street silhouettes. Each drop is produced in limited quantities—once it’s gone, it’s gone.

Materials

Heavyweight cotton, 925 silver, and hardware tested for real wear.

Process

Illustrations → screens → fabric → finishing. Small-batch, never mass.

Values

Quality over quantity. Story over hype. Community over clout.
