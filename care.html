Care & Craftsmanship

Luxury is in the details. Each piece is designed, crafted, and finished with intention.

Materials

We source only premium textiles, metals, and stones. From heavyweight cottons and tailored cuts to VVS1 diamonds and 925 sterling silver, every choice reflects our commitment to luxury that lasts.

Craft

Hand Finishing

Each garment undergoes manual inspection and finishing, ensuring precision down to every stitch.

Jewelry Detailing

Our pendants, rings, and accents are built like heirlooms, with metals and stones meant to last generations.

Limited Runs

We release in small batches to preserve exclusivity. When it’s gone, it’s gone — no restocks.

Care Instructions

  • Hand wash or cold machine wash garments inside out. Hang dry only.
  • Keep jewelry away from harsh chemicals; polish with a soft cloth.
  • Store pieces in dust bags or cases to protect finishes.

Our Promise

Every piece of Sins of the Past carries our signature — quality, meaning, and story. This is not fast fashion. This is legacy wear.
